Le Lorem

A Paris destination in its own right, Le Lorem by Christian Le Fer is synonymous with the apex of French cuisine and the combination of classic ingredients with ambitious, new techniques. Its three Michelin stars are a reflection of the gastronomic experience of a lifetime.

L'Ipsum

The elegant designed dining room reflects the menu of our Michelin-starred restaurant – light and modern, Mediterranean-style cuisine, designed for sharing and bursting with freshness.

L’Orangerie

This art-deco–inspired, glass-and-steel structure houses a contemporary restaurant in the very best Parisian style, with a Michelin-star menu of traditional French cuisine made new again and a terrace that draws the city’s most chic crowd for langourous lunches.

La Galerie

The heart of the Hotel, our all-day lounge is festooned with 19th-century art and Flemish tapestries, the perfect spot for post-shopping tea, a mid-day coffee break or a summer cocktail in the courtyard.

Le Bar

Intimate, atmospheric and perfect for a rendezvous, our cosy bar draws in guests throughout the day for chats over coffee, sundowners and digestifs.

In-room Dining

Whether you’re craving a morning croissant or a steak and bottle of Burgundy from Le Cinq, our in-room dining service delivers a gourmet experience to your room or suite, 24 hours a day.
